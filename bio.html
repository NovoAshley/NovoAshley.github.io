About Me

Ashley Novo was born into a Spanish, Japanese and Colombian family in Staten Island, New York City. She is a self-taught videographer, editor, screenwriter and director with four younger siblings who are frequently featured in her videos. Ashley is planning on obtaining the 5-year Bachelor of Architecture degree at Iowa State University, using her eye for the camera to help develop the design of physical structures.
